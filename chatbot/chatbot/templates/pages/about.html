About The Chatbot

Your trusted source for quick and reliable medical feedback.

Welcome to the General Practice Chatbot Application—a quick and accessible way to receive legitimate medical information based on your symptoms. Our website is designed for people seeking fast, reliable medical feedback, all in a secure and user-friendly environment.

Our chatbot application is built with a strong foundation in Python using the Django framework, ensuring a robust and dynamic experience. We’ve integrated machine learning technology to interpret user inputs effectively, creating a more tailored response to your questions. On the frontend, we use Bootstrap to provide a clean, interactive interface that’s easy to navigate, regardless of your tech skills.

Features

  • Secure login portal for privacy and session management.
  • Backend database for storing and managing relevant information.
  • Natural language processing (NLP) to understand questions better and provide accurate responses.

To maintain our high standards, we’re committed to quality through a structured testing plan that identifies usability and performance improvements.
